Autonomous 3D Drone Mapping System

The Problem

Countless lives are lost not just during a natural disaster, but in their aftermath. Lack of effective search equipment and inability to enter hazardous open areas with complex infrastructure makes it difficult to detect survivors among the ruins.

This project seeks to bridge this gap by integrating AI-powered human detection directly into 3D SLAM mapping, enabling real-time semantic understanding of environments for faster, safer rescue operations.
